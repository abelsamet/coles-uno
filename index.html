Cole's Uno

Cole's own version of Uno. Same rules you know, plus three brand new cards Cole invented. Pick who you are playing against to start.

Wild Draw 5 / 10 / 20: pick a color and the other player draws that many cards. The Draw 20 can end a game on its own.

Trash card: play it when its color matches the pile, and dump every card of that color from your hand at once. The buried cards do nothing, the trash card is what counts.

Uno: when you get down to one card, tap the glowing Uno button fast. Forget, and you draw 2.
